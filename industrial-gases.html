Industrial Gases

Industrial Compressed Oxygen Gas


We supply compressed Oxygen gas produced from one of our two air separation units and compressed into cylinders at a pressure of up to 200 bar (3000 psi). Oxygen can be considered an oxidizing agent in various applications. Its Chemical, and Physical properties are Oxidizing, Colorless, Odorless. and Tasteless Gas. Its source is air separation units.



Uses of Gases by Industry:


  • Steel fabrications industry “Cutting, Welding & Brazing”
  • Aquaculture
  • Oxidation and fermentation industry
  • Water treatment



Industrial Compressed Nitrogen Gas


We supply compressed Nitrogen gas produced from two air separation units. Its Chemical, and Physical properties are Odorless, Colorless, Non-Flammable, and Non-Toxic. Its source is air separation units.



Uses of Gases by Industry:


  • Inerting and Purging
  • Leak Pressure Testing
  • Steel Laser Cutting
  • Actuator Gas



Industrial Compressed Argon Gas


We supply compressed Argon produced from one of our two air separation units and compressed into cylinders at a pressure of up to 200 bar (3000 psi). Argon is considered One of the Rare Gases. Its Chemical, and Physical properties are Inert, Colorless, odorless, non-toxic, and Present in an atmosphere of just less than one percent. Its source is air separation units.



Uses of Gases by Industry:


  • Steel fabrication industry, Welding as a shielding gas for MIG/MAG & TIG Processes
  • Steel Making
  • Fluorescent light tubes & Bulbs
  • Glass Panels
